Feli

Name: Felix

Nicknames: Feli, Baby, Schnuckiputz

Warrior name: Pinknose → Dirtynose

Gender: female(!)

Age: 15 years

Colour: black and white

Likes: attention, watching birds, wall heating

Dislikes: other cats, cutlery noises, finishing her food

My family got Feli as a kitten when I was 8 years old. We gave her a male name because we thought she was a boy! She grew up with me, and I love her very much, but she can be a bitch sometimes (she loves to bite and scratch you!). She doesn't like being picked up anymore, and she's getting a bit old, but except for a bad hip she's still as healthy as ever! Feli hates other cats and is very territorial about our patio (with a little help from my mom). And she meows. A lot. She is trained, and knows the command for sit, give paw, high five, and beg (stand up on hind legs).
