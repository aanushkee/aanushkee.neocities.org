Faq

Commissions/Requests? No, sorry!

Software? I draw in Krita (PC) or AutoDesk Sketchbook (Android) and edit in Photoshop.

Hardware? A really cheap graphic tablet for PC, and a Samsung tablet for Android.

Can I use? You may use any and all of my art for any personal use (e.g. profile pictures) without asking, as long as you credit me and make no money with it.

Can I (re-)post? Feel free to post anywhere with credit & link back to me, no need to ask.
