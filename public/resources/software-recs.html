Software Recommendations

Some neat programs for your enjoyment. All are free. I use all of these on a regular or semi-regular basis and think they are awesome enough to be featured here.

Programs for Windows

FreeCam
screen recorder
Krita
free drawing/painting software
MPC-BE
great video player with support multiple audio tracks, subtitles, etc.
Notion
amazing note-taking tool, i use it for uni
Plex + Media Server
stream video files stored on your pc from any other device (including your smart tv, via the Plex app on your tv!). adds posters, episode titles, etc if you follow their file naming conventions.
ScreenToGif
record your screen and easily turn it into a gif, has basic gif editing features
TeamViewer
remotely control your own or a friend's pc
Voicemeter
audio mixer, makes it possible to connext 2 bluetooth earbuds/headphones to one device at the same time
VSCode
the best editor for coding.
Check my coding setup page for a list of extensions I recommend.

Regarding files:

Bulk Rename Utility
quickly change multiple file/folder names at once
Calibre
e-book manager
FreeFileSync
great tool for backups (it automatically detects differences between two folders and only updates what is necessary, which means it takes 1 minute instead of 1 hour to transfer files!)
HandBrake
convert video files (file type, quality, etc)
TreeSize Free
quickly see which folders/files on your pc take up the most space. great for making space on your hard drive or preparing for a backup.

Firefox Add-ons

Some/Most of these are probably also available for other Browsers.

FireShot
take a screenshot of a full webpage (it scrolls for you)
G-Translate on Right Click
adds a 'translate with google translate' option to the right-click menu
Greasemonkey
makes it possible to use userscripts such as:
  • hide youtube shorts
  • ao3 kudos/hits ratio
  • letterboxd imdb ratings
  • my userscripts
Stylus
makes it possible to use userstyles (CSS code effecting any website).
(Note: there is a similar extension called Stylish that you should not use because of privacy concerns!)
Tabby Cat
a random cute cat for your 'new tab' screen
YouTube Video and Audio Downloader
what is says on the tin, and it actually works

Android Apps

Some/Most of these are probably also available for iOS.

AutoDesk Sketchbook
best drawing app for Android
Brave Browser
great, secure browser; good for streaming sites
Bring
shopping list app with great features such as autmatic icons, reordering of categories, and syncing with others
PureWriter
my favorite writing app
Remini
make low-quality pictures higher-quality
Smart AudioBook Player
the best player for audiobooks you have stored as files, has many features
Via Browser
great minimalistic browser
Zedge
great app to find custom ringtones and notification sounds (e.g. from movies, games, etc)

Phone Detox

For those of you who want to become less addicted to your phone:

AppUp
tells you how time you've spent using certain apps
StayFree
the best app to set yourself time limits for scrolling, focus mode, and more
NoScroll
blocks all tiktoks, youtube shorts, and instagram reels
